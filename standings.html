Standings

What do these columns mean?
  • Preds: how many of the already-locked matches this player actually predicted, out of the total locked so far.
  • Exact: matches where the player guessed the exact final score.
  • Match pts: points earned from match predictions (outcome tier × phase multiplier), summed across every scored match.
  • Special: combined points from the champion and top scorer picks.
  • Total: Match pts + Special.
  • ± Avg: how many points above (green, +) or below (red, −) the group's average total.
