What do these columns mean?
  • Predictions submitted: how many of the already-locked matches this user actually predicted, out of the total locked so far.
  • Exact hits: matches where the user guessed the exact final score.
  • vs. Average (pts): how far this user's total is above (green, +) or below (red, -) the group's average total.
  • Matches (pts): points earned from match predictions (outcome tier × phase multiplier), summed across every scored match.
  • Special (pts): combined points from the champion and top scorer picks.
  • Total (pts): Matches (pts) + Special (pts).
